# Scope
 
This document summarises the processes and procedures contained within the Softwerx Security Operations team's playbooks, manuals, and framework documents.
 
The scope of the secure365 MDR service covers:
 
- Continuous monitoring of customer environments via Microsoft Sentinel
- Detection and triage of security events, alerts, and incidents
- Escalation and communication procedures for confirmed incidents
- Roles, responsibilities, and accountability across the SOC team
- Processes that govern how the SOC operates day to day
