## Customer Contacts
 
- A minimum of **two telephone contact numbers** are provided by each customer
- These numbers are contacted in the event of a **P1 incident** that has passed the threshold for a positive determination
- Email addresses or Teams channels are used for all other communication
- Each customer is assigned a dedicated **Softwerx account manager**
