# Mission Statement
 
## SOC Mission
 
The mission of the Security Operations team is to:
 
- Identify and alert on cyber threats and compliance exceptions
- Assist in minimising the impact of adverse events that do occur
- Achieve rapid situational awareness of current threats and compliance statutes
- Automate manual reporting processes
- Expedite remediation and incident response times
- Continually measure and improve the effectiveness of security and compliance processes
- Automate monitoring and enforcement of security controls
 
---
 
## DevOps Mission
 
To architect, automate, and operationalise the security infrastructure, integrations, and intelligence systems that enable the secure365 service to detect threats rapidly, respond effectively, and scale securely — while reducing operational toil, optimising costs, and continuously improving detection capabilities across our managed Microsoft security services.
 
### What We Do
 
We architect and maintain the technology foundation that the Security Operations Centre relies on. We develop automation to eliminate manual toil, integrate external data sources for richer threat intelligence, and ensure our systems perform reliably at scale.
 
Our DevOps team enables this mission by:
 
- Building the infrastructure, platforms, and automation layers that support detection and response
- Ensuring Sentinel and Defender systems operate at peak performance and scale
- Developing integrations and orchestration that reduce manual analyst toil
- Implementing data enrichment and correlation capabilities
- Creating the tools, dashboards, and automation that SOC teams depend on
- Optimising costs and data ingestion strategies to maximise efficiency
- Translating security data into actionable business intelligence using Power BI
 
---
 
## Sub-Department Mission Statements
 
### SecOps Engineers
 
**Mission:** To deploy, maintain, and optimise the foundational infrastructure, data connectors, and systems that support the secure365 service — ensuring reliable, scalable, and cost-effective Microsoft Sentinel and Defender environments across all managed customers.
 
**Responsibilities:**
 
- Design and implement Sentinel workspace architecture, tiering strategies, and data ingestion optimisation
- Manage and troubleshoot data connector health and performance (firewalls, network devices, key vaults, storage accounts, antivirus, etc.)
- Perform administrative, backup, disaster recovery, and operational procedures for Microsoft Sentinel
- Support customer onboarding by configuring secure, scalable Sentinel deployments
- Interface with SOC analysts to address infrastructure issues and optimise platform performance
- Manage cost optimisation through Data Lake tiering and data retention strategies
- Automate routine SOC tasks including alert correlation, false positive filtering, enrichment, and escalation
- Optimise Sentinel queries and automation for performance and accuracy
- Support customers with ad-hoc requests related to the secure365 service
 
---
 
### SecOps Developers
 
**Mission:** To eliminate manual SOC toil and expand detection capabilities through intelligent automation, low-code integrations, and SOAR orchestration — accelerating incident response, enriching investigative data, and enabling the secure365 SOC to scale detection coverage without proportional staffing increases.
 
**Responsibilities:**
 
- Develop Azure Logic Apps workflows for alert enrichment, notification, and data gathering automation
- Create Sentinel playbooks for incident orchestration and automated response actions
- Design and implement third-party data integrations (Have I Been Pwned, external threat feeds, enrichment APIs)
- Develop low-code solutions using Azure resources and online service APIs
- Build custom integrations between Sentinel and external systems for expanded data collection
- Work closely with SOC analysts to understand pain points and automate repetitive tasks
- Create and maintain automation runbooks and documentation
- Automate the secure365 onboarding process
 
---
 
### Power BI Developers
 
**Mission:** To deliver actionable business and operational intelligence through analytics dashboards — providing leadership, customers, and SOC teams with real-time visibility into service quality, security posture, financial performance, and operational effectiveness.
 
**Responsibilities:**
 
- Design and develop secure365 analytics dashboards with KPIs relevant to customers and leadership
- Create visualisations for incident metrics including total incidents, true positives, false positives, and MTTR
- Develop operational dashboards for SOC team performance and efficiency metrics
- Build customer-facing dashboards that demonstrate service value and security outcomes
- Implement data quality checks and refresh schedules for dashboard reliability
- Create reports that support process improvement and continuous optimisation efforts
- Continuously identify opportunities for efficiency improvements and implementation of new data sources
